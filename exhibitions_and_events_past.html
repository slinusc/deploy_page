The Afterlife of Technology

What happens when the tools of innovation become obsolete?
This exhibition explores the lifecycle of technology, transforming discarded screens and hardware into a powerful commentary on consumption and decay. Through this striking installation, visitors are invited to confront the environmental and cultural consequences of our digital age.
