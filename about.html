About the Museum

No. 88, Section 5, Roosevelt Road, Wenshan District, Taipei City, Taiwan 116

Modern AI is a groundbreaking museum exploring the intersection of humanity, technology, and the environment through art and innovation. With immersive exhibits and speculative storytelling, it challenges visitors to confront the consequences of unchecked technological progress and environmental neglect. By blending vivid imagery and interactive experiences, the museum inspires reflection on our choices today and their impact on future generations. Modern AI bridges speculative futures with tangible solutions, empowering visitors to imagine and shape a sustainable tomorrow.
